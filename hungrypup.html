hungry pup

Hungry Pup is a short film about a puppy's little adventure. The illustrations and scenes were all created in Adobe Illustrator. However the title sequence was illustrated in Adobe After Effects. The star puppy in the video was filmed with a green screen. Both the footage and illustrations were put together in Adobe After Effects and Adobe Premiere.
