plumby pipe

Plumby Pipe is a puzzle game genre for mobile phones. The player needs to connect the bottom pipes to the top or side pipes. The more pipes the player connects the higher the score. Although the player would need to watch out for how many steps they have left because once the player is out of steps, the game is over. Also as the game progresses, boulders and pipes will pop up to block the way. The goal of this game is to achieve the highest score and to unlock all the pipes.

My role was to create all the graphic elements, the user interface, and the trailer.

This game was featured by Apple and has made it to the top 50 charts in the Canadian app store for best new games.

process

I have worked on this game for about 4 months. I was told to create one background theme, but instead I created three to choose from; street fire, bathroom and veggie garden. I have also created animation sprites for each of the three themes. For street fire, the animation was a firefighter trying to put out the building fire. As for the bathroom theme, the animated characters could interact with the bathtub/shower, the sink and the toilet. Lastly for the veggie garden the players would be watering the plants whenever they connect the pipes. As a result, the game developers chose the veggie garden theme because they found it relaxing.
