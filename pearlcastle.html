pearl castle café promo

I created a promotion poster for a restaurant called Pearl Castle Café celebrating their 2 year anniversary. This design portrays a clean and simple look while highlighting the important information. I utilized icons to establish hierarchy. This way it helps guide the customers of what to look first. For example, if they are a customer of two, the customers would see the two people icon and read what is there.

pearl castle café special lunch menu

The goal was to create a special lunch menu for Pearl Castle Café, a Taiwanese restaurant. The client wanted the menus to look bright and summer like. Since it was a Taiwanese restaurant, there needs to be a mandarin version of the menu as well. However I think if both english and mandarin were all on one page it would look to clustered, thus I made them separate.
