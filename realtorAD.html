realtor advertisment

I was approached by Roy Kai Kang, a realtor, who requested an advertising flyer to promote himself to a Chinese audience with the description of his personal trait. My goal was to create a visually appealing ad for the client in real estate.

process

I created multiple flyers for the client to choose from. The visual hierarchy was important for an advertising flyer. Therefore the slogan and the phone number are what the audience should see first. I used minimalistic colours for a clean and professional look.
